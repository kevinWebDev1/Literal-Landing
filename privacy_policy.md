# Privacy Policy for Literal

**Last Updated:** March 01, 2026

Literal ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your personal information is collected, used, and disclosed by Literal when you use our mobile application (the "App").

## 1. Information We Collect

To provide our core accountability and app-locking services, Literal must collect certain types of data.

### A. Personal Information
- **Account Data:** When you register, we collect your email address, display name, and authentication tokens (via Google Sign-In or other providers).
- **Keyholder Connections:** Information regarding the "Ward" and "Keyholder" relationships you establish within the app.

### B. Device and Usage Data
- **App Usage Statistics:** We collect data on which applications you open, how long you use them, and your locked/unlocked status. This is strictly to enforce your quotas and generate accountability reports.
- **Device Information:** We may collect non-identifiable device information (e.g., OS version, device model) for debugging and crash reporting.

### C. Sensitive Permissions
Literal utilizes several sensitive Android permissions to function effectively:

**1. Accessibility Services API**
- **Why we use it:** This service is required to detect which app is currently active on your screen so Literal can enforce app locks, block restricted content (e.g., Porn Addiction Mode), and track your usage quotas accurately. When Porn Addiction Mode is enabled, the accessibility service also scans screen text to detect and block explicit URLs or keywords.
- **What we do NOT do:** We do **not** use Accessibility Services to record your screen, read your passwords, intercept personal messages, or collect data outside the scope of app usage tracking and content blocking. Screen text is scanned locally on your device and is never transmitted to our servers.

**2. Query All Packages (Installed Apps)**
- **Why we use it:** Literal queries your device's installed applications to dynamically identify web browsers. This allows the app to effectively apply content filters across all browsers without tracking your specific app names online.
- **What we do NOT do:** We do not upload or share your list of installed applications with third parties.

### D. Tokens and Commitments
- We store data regarding your in-app tokens, staked commitments, and success/failure rates relating to those commitments.

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:
- **Core Functionality:** To lock apps, enforce quotas, and manage the Ward/Keyholder accountability system.
- **Notifications:** To send push notifications regarding unlock requests, emergencies, and commitment failures.
- **Improvement:** To analyze usage trends, fix bugs, and improve the App's performance.
- **Fraud Prevention:** To detect bypass attempts or unauthorized uninstalls.

## 3. How We Share Your Information

Your privacy is our priority. **We do not sell your personal data.** 
We only share information in the following circumstances:
- **With Your Keyholder:** If you attach a Keyholder to your account, they will receive notifications and reports regarding your app usage, unlock requests, and commitment statuses.
- **Third-Party Service Providers:** We use trusted third-party services (such as Firebase for database hosting, Vercel for backend notifications, and Google for authentication) who only process your data to power the App's features securely.
- **Legal Compliance:** If required by law, subpoena, or other legal processes.

## 4. Data Retention

We retain your Personal Information and Usage Data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you with the App's services. If you delete your account, your data will be permanently removed from our active databases, except where retention is required by law.

## 5. Your Rights and Data Deletion

You have the right to access, update, or delete your information at any time.

**How to Request Data Deletion:**
You can request the complete deletion of your account and all associated data by:
1. Using the "Delete Account" option within the App's settings menu (if available).
2. Contacting our support team at literal.app.business@gmail.com.

Upon a verified deletion request, we will erase your account data, usage history, and token balances from our servers within 30 days.

## 6. Children's Privacy
Our App is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us to have it removed.
